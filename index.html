About Us

Our goal is to let passionate sky divers like us experience the beauty of the world. The Sky Divers club in Monash is establised since 2020 December to help club members to explore Melbourne. We are based in Melbourne and have brought passionate sky diver like you explore different part of Melbourne. We have collaboration with few of the skydiving team across Melbourne to bring this benefits to you. So what are you waiting for? Come sign up and be a part of the family. To know more about the club, you can call us or email us and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Things You Need To Know

These are the common questions we get from our club members

  • Age limit and weight limit

    By law, the minimum age is 12 years old but if you are under 18 years old, a parent or legal guardian must be present on the day of the jump. The weight limit is 115kg.

  • Attire Requirement

    Loose, casual clothing with fully enclosed shoes is recommeded. For example, running or sports trainers. Hiking boots and high heels are not appropriate for this activity.

  • Free Transportation

    Our club provides free transportation to the sky diving center. However, you have to meet us at Monash University if you want free transportation to the center.

  • How to Join the Club?

    You can join our club by entering your details at the footer below or you can email us which is SkyDiversMonash@gmail.com.
